About Orlena

Orlena is a dessert brownie brand from Bali, established in 2018 under the umbrella of PT Orlena Delapan Mulia. With a strong commitment to quality and innovation, Orlena offers a variety of brownies and desserts featuring distinctive flavors that have become favorites among both locals and tourists.
We believe that every bite should leave a lasting impression—this is why we continuously innovate in our recipes, packaging, and customer experience.
Today, Orlena operates multiple branches and growing online distribution channels. Our vision is to become the leading brownie brand in Indonesia, and our mission is to spread happiness through sweet products made with love.

Orlena's History

Orlena was founded in 2018 by Metta Puspita at the age of 15, starting from a small home kitchen selling brownies online. Her passion for desserts and drive for innovation led to a signature product known for its rich flavor, fudgy texture, and eye-catching look.
As demand grew, Orlena opened its first outlet in Bali and expanded its team. The brand continued to evolve—improving products, packaging, and service—to stand out in a competitive market.
In 2025, Orlena became part of PT. Orlena Delapan Mulia, reflecting a stronger commitment to long-term growth and professional management. Today, it is more than just a favorite Bali treat —it represents quality, creativity, and consistency.
Looking ahead, Orlena is set to expand further—sharing happiness through brownies, one bite at a time.

Orlena CSR 2025: A Brighter Future for SDN 5 Munduk

In 2025, Orlena took a heartfelt step beyond the kitchen—into the classroom. As part of our CSR mission, we helped restore SDN 5 Munduk, a small elementary school nestled in the hills of North Bali. More than repairing walls and repainting classrooms, this project was about rebuilding hope—creating a safe, inspiring space where children can dream, learn, and grow. We upgraded learning facilities, donated educational tools, and supported environmental programs that rooted awareness from an early age. This is our way of giving back to the island that shaped us. Because at Orlena, we believe that nurturing futures is just as essential as creating comfort through food.
