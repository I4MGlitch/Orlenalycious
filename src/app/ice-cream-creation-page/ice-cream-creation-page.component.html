Ice Cream Creation

Creating ice cream creations as a complementary offering to our main desserts, such as milkshakes and soft serve, is a fantastic way to enhance our menu and provide customers with a wellrounded dessert experience. Here's how we can craft these ice cream creations featuring or signature brownies:

  • Milkshakes, we make a milkshakes with 5 option of toppings. Chocolate Brownies, Greentea Brownies, Lotus Biscoff, Oreo, Nutella.
  • Soft Cream, we also make another dessert experience by mixing our signature brownies with ice cream. We have 2 variants, soft serve choco brownies and greentea brownies
